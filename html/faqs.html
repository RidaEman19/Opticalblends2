Frequently Asked Questions (Festivals)

Diwali is the Hindu festival of lights, celebrated with diyas, fireworks, sweets, and prayers to Goddess Lakshmi.

Holi celebrates the arrival of spring and the victory of good over evil, marked by playing with vibrant colors.

Eid-ul-Fitr marks the end of Ramadan, and is celebrated with prayers, feasting, giving charity, and family visits.

Eid-ul-Adha is the festival of sacrifice, honoring the willingness of Prophet Ibrahim to sacrifice his son for God.

Christmas celebrates the birth of Jesus Christ, with traditions like gift-giving, decorations, and church services.

Easter celebrates the resurrection of Jesus Christ and is marked by prayers, family meals, and Easter eggs.
