ISLAM

Peace • Prayer • Unity

Eid ul-Fitr

The "Festival of Breaking the Fast" marks the end of Ramadan. It is a day of joy, special prayers, and charity (Zakat al-Fitr).

Eid ul-Adha

The "Festival of Sacrifice" honors Prophet Ibrahim’s willingness to sacrifice his son. Meat is shared with family and the poor.

Ramadan

Ramadan is the holy month of fasting, prayer, and reflection observed by Muslims worldwide. It is a time of spiritual growth, self-discipline, and community, marked by fasting from dawn to sunset.

CHRISTIANITY

Faith • Hope • Love

Christmas

December 25. The birth of Jesus Christ is celebrated with lights, carols, gifts, and prayers.

Easter

Marks the resurrection of Jesus. It stands for new life and victory over death.

Good Friday

The day Jesus was crucified. Observed with fasting and prayers in churches.

Palm Sunday

Begins Holy Week and remembers Jesus entering Jerusalem.

HINDUISM

Light • Color • Devotion

Diwali

The Festival of Lights. It shows victory of light over darkness and good over evil.

Holi

The Festival of Colors. Celebrated with joy, colors, and the arrival of spring.

Karwa Chauth

Women fast from sunrise to moonrise for their husbands' safety and long life.

Navratri

Nine nights of devotion to Goddess Durga — symbolizing power and purity.
